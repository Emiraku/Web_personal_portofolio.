How Does Artificial Intelligence Work?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science that focuses on building systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, problem-solving, decision-making, and understanding natural language.

AI systems are built using algorithms and models that process large amounts of data to find patterns. One of the most popular subsets of AI is Machine Learning, which allows computers to learn and improve their performance without being explicitly programmed.

Applications of AI include virtual assistants (like Siri and Alexa), autonomous vehicles, recommendation systems (like Netflix and Spotify), and more. The possibilities are expanding as research in this field continues to evolve.
